## Tax Credits & Financial Incentives

### Federal Disabled Access Credit (IRS Form 8826)
- 50% of eligible expenses between $250-$10,250 → max $5,000/year
- Small businesses only (<$1M revenue or ≤30 employees)
- Source: [IRS Form 8826](https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-8826)

### Federal Barrier Removal Deduction (IRC §190)
- Up to $15,000/year deduction for any business
- Covers architectural barrier removal
- Source: [ADA.gov Tax Incentives](https://archive.ada.gov/taxcred.htm)

### California CASp Credit
- $250 state tax credit after CASp inspection
- Source: [CA DOR Credits](https://www.dor.ca.gov/Home/CreditsandIncentives)

### Combined: up to $20,250/year in accessibility cost offsets

## AI for Physical Accessibility — Precedents

- Lawrence, KS: lidar + GIS for 6,500 curb ramps, $100M ADA transition plan
- Douglas County/Omaha: deep learning on aerial imagery for 30,000+ curb ramps
- Source: [GovTech](https://www.govtech.com/infrastructure/improving-ada-compliance-in-the-built-environment-with-gis-ai)
- Drexel University: computer vision for building inspection (concrete cracks)
- Source: [ScienceDaily](https://sciencedaily.com/releases/2024/01/240130133547.htm)
