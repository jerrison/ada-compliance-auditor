## Unruh Civil Rights Act (California)

- **$4,000 minimum statutory damages per violation per occasion**
- Codified at California Civil Code Section 52(a)
- Each visit = separate occasion (3 visits = $12,000+ minimum)
- ADA violation automatically = Unruh violation (no intent required)
- Statute: [Cal. Civ. Code §52](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?sectionNum=52.&lawCode=CIV)
- Explainer: [Jeffer Mangels ADA Basics](https://ada.jeffer.com/california-unruh-civil-rights-act-law-basics/)

## Qualified Defendant Protection

- CASp inspection before being served reduces minimum from $4,000 to $1,000 (75% reduction)
- Fewer than 1% of defendants activated this in 2024 (42 of 4,623 cases)
- Source: [CASp California](https://www.caspcalifornia.com/resources/unruh-civil-rights-act)

## Who Gets Sued Most

- Restaurant/food service: 45.36% of all 2024 CA filings (2,340 cases)
- Parking violations are #1 — top 4 violations all relate to parking
- Source: [CASp California](https://www.caspcalifornia.com/resources/unruh-civil-rights-act)
- Most targeted: restaurants, retail, coffee shops, breweries, hotels, auto repair
- Source: [CBS News SF](https://www.cbsnews.com/sanfrancisco/news/serial-plaintiff-turns-california-ada-lawsuits-into-lucrative-cottage-industry/)

## Pro Se / AI-Assisted Filing Trends

- 40% of federal ADA Title III filings are now pro se
- 40% more pro se ADA lawsuits in 2025 vs 2024
- 2024: 1,774 federal pro se; 2025: 1,867 in first 9 months
- Plaintiffs use ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini to draft complaints
- Source: [Seyfarth Shaw](https://www.adatitleiii.com/2025/10/federal-pro-se-ada-title-iii-and-fha-lawsuit-numbers-surge-likely-powered-by-ai/)
